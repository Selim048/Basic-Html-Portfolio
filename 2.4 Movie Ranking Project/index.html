Selim's Favorite Movies!

My favorite movies of all time:

The Pianist

This movie lands on top of my list, as it made me cry so much
and I feel like I have lived through similar problems myself.
The director did a great job in making the show realistic and
really showing the feelings of the poor pianist. With the main
role being one of my favorites, this movie gets a 10/10 from me!


The Book of Life

This movie is a big part of my childhood. I loved it so much that
I would come back again and again to watch it. The music
was great, the message was very sweet and the plot was done
justice. As I was a small kid then I was really invested in
the love plot as well. This movie is a great watch as a fantasy movie
even for adults and I would 100 precent recommend it to everyone!


The Wind Rises

Even if the world of animation has progressed to greater lengths, this
movie still remains a masterpiece. It comes with great colours mixed with
incredible music and a great story. You might see a pattern with me and dramas,
but I promise you will not regret watching this movie in particular. So even
if you are not a big fan of anime, give the productions of studio ghibli a try.
